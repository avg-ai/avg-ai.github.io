Frequently Asked Questions

What business problems do you solve with automation and chatbots?

I help businesses eliminate routine tasks and improve communication. For example, I automate lead capture from websites or ads into your CRM, set up notifications for key events in Slack or Telegram, sync data between spreadsheets and tools, and build AI-powered chatbots that answer customer questions 24/7. The main goal is to save your time so you can grow your business.

How does the project workflow look like?

It’s simple: we connect to discuss your task and goals. I suggest a solution and estimate the cost and timeline. Once agreed, I start building and testing the automation or chatbot. You get a ready-to-use solution and clear instructions. If needed, I request access to relevant services — all shared securely.

How much does it cost?

The price depends on the complexity of the task — how many services are connected, how many steps are involved, and whether AI or custom logic is needed. Simple projects start at $100. More complex ones are priced individually. Just drop me a message and I’ll give you a precise estimate.

Do I need paid accounts for Zapier or other tools? What about support?

Zapier has a free plan, but many business needs require a paid one — we’ll figure that out during planning. You’ll also need accounts on the services we’ll connect. After delivery, I offer 14 days of free support to fix any issues on my side. Expanding features or adding new tasks can be discussed separately.

Do you work with platforms other than Zapier?

Yes, absolutely. I can build automation and chatbots on other platforms too — like n8n (even self-hosted), ManyChat, Dialogflow, Make, Pabbly Connect, Tidio, Albato, Activepieces, and more. We’ll choose the right tool depending on your needs and preferences.
